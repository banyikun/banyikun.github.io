Yikun Ban


        Hey! you can directly call me Ban ~
I am a Second-year Phd student in computer science at University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign, advised by Jinrui He. Prior to this, I obtained my Master degree from @ EECS, Peking University and bachelor from Wuhan University.
My current research studies the multi-armed bandit combined with data mining and deep learning. In the past, my research efforts have included bussness data modeling, upper confidence bound, regret analysis, similarity metric designing, clustering algorithms, and graph mining algorithms.
